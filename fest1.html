Christmas-Birth of Jesus Christ

The birth of jesus christ is celebrated with ecstatic marriment and wonderful joy, throughout india. The warm smell of walnut cakes can be inhaled in every household. as if captivated by a confetti of santa caps, Christmas sees the prevalence of these caps and reindeer headgear decorating the heads civilians. Many people decorate Christmas trees in their homes with colourful stars and handing balls. people also visit the church to seek the holy blessings of christ on this auspicious festival.
